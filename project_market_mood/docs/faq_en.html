# FAQ
I’m not receiving notifications.
Please check if notifications are turned on in the app settings, if your thresholds and time options are set as you want, and if notification permission is granted.

If it has been less than one hour since you installed the app, notifications may not arrive yet.

Some devices may automatically put the app into “sleep mode” after long periods of inactivity, which can block notifications.

Check your device manufacturer’s instructions on how to exclude the app from sleep mode. Opening the app at least once a week also helps prevent the system from treating it as inactive.
Where does the data come from?
This app visualizes market indicators that are publicly provided by CNN Business and FRED. It is not an official service of CNN Business or FRED, but an independent third-party app that utilizes publicly available data.
Is the data updated in real time?
No. This app displays the latest data provided by CNN Business and FRED, but each source updates its data every few minutes or on a daily basis.

You can check the update time in the [Data Timestamp] widget on the main screen, and tap it for more detailed information.

Before making any important decisions, please make sure the information in this app is up to date.
The data is not displayed.
First, check that your network connection is stable, then completely close and restart the app.

Even with a stable connection, data may not appear due to the data providers’ conditions. In this case, please wait a while and try launching the app again later.
Do you collect personal information?
No. This app has no login feature and does not collect any personal information from users.
Are there any extra charges?
No. This app is sold as a paid app, and there are no in-app purchases or ads.
When does the market sentiment image change?
The market sentiment image changes according to the 5 levels of the Fear & Greed Index.
