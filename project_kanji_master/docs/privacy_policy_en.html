Privacy Policy

This app does not directly collect personal information from users. Below we explain our policies and your rights regarding privacy.

1. General

We value the protection of users’ personal information. This Privacy Policy applies when you use this app. By installing, accessing, or using the app, you agree to this Policy.

2. Collection of Personal Information

  • This app does not provide account registration or social login features.
  • We do not collect directly identifiable personal information such as name, email address, or contact details.
  • For the purpose of improving service quality, anonymous technical data such as usage patterns may be collected through Firebase Analytics.

3. Storage of Learning Data

  • Learning progress, review history, and related data are stored locally on the user’s device.
  • Users may choose to back up their learning data to Google Drive or iCloud.
  • Backup data does not include directly identifiable personal information such as name or email address.
  • Cloud backups are performed at the user’s discretion, and the processing and security of data in those services are governed by the respective platform’s policies.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

Since this app does not directly collect personal information, we do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. However, when users choose to use cloud backup (Google Drive or iCloud), the respective platform may process technical information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

5. Technical Information (Diagnostics and Analytics)

This app uses Google Firebase Analytics to improve service quality and analyze usage patterns. Through this service, app usage data, error logs, device information, app version, and country-level location information may be automatically collected.

The information collected does not include directly identifiable personal information such as name or email address. Such data is processed in accordance with Google Firebase Analytics’ privacy policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

As this app does not directly collect personal information, there are no separate procedures for storing or deleting such data. Learning data stored within the app will be removed when the app is deleted from the user’s device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page and will become effective immediately upon publication. Continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

8. Contact

For inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy: contact@freenaru.com
